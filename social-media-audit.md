# Social Media Audit: GoCharting
**Date:** April 17, 2026

## Overview
GoCharting's social presence is highly technical and aligned with its "Professional Trader" audience. It serves as a middle-to-bottom funnel tool, focusing on education and high-signal market analysis.

---

## 1. Platform Performance

### YouTube ([@GoCharting](https://www.youtube.com/@GoCharting))
*   **Pillars:** Educational deep-dives, "How-to" webinars, and feature walk-throughs (Orderflow, DOM, Lipi).
*   **Tone:** Highly authoritative and technical.
*   **Engagement:** Strongest on videos covering "Orderflow Analysis" and "Market Profile."
*   **CTA:** Consistent links to Discord and Pro Plans in descriptions.

### X (Twitter) ([@gocharting](https://x.com/gocharting))
*   **Pillars:** Real-time chart setups (CVD, Delta, Liquidity Grabs), product updates, and user-generated chart retweets.
*   **Tone:** "Pro-to-Pro" communication. No "hype" language.
*   **Engagement:** High on "Before & After" trade setups showing absorption or liquidity sweeps.
*   **Visuals:** High-quality, dark-mode chart screenshots that reinforce the "Pro" brand.

---

## 2. Brand Alignment Audit
*   **Technical (✅):** Content assumes a high level of market knowledge.
*   **Authoritative (✅):** Positioned as the expert in Orderflow.
*   **Empowering (✅):** Heavy focus on teaching users how to build their own tools via Lipi.

---

## 3. Prioritized Recommendations
1.  **Short-form Repurposing:** Convert 60-minute webinars into 60-second "Chart Tips" for YouTube Shorts and X.
2.  **Lipi Spotlight:** Create a "Script of the Week" series to showcase differentiation from TradingView.
3.  **"Why I Switched" Series:** Use social proof/testimonials of traders moving from QuantTower or TradingView.
4.  **Pinned High-Value Post:** A "Start Here" thread on X explaining the Unified Ecosystem (Charting + Execution + Lipi).

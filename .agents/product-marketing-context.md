# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: April 17, 2026*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** A multi-asset orderflow charting and trading platform for advanced market analysis.
**What it does:** Provides deep-tier market transparency through Orderflow, Market Profile, and Footprint charts, while allowing direct execution via multi-broker integrations.
**Product category:** Unified Trading & Orderflow Analytics Platform.
**Product type:** SaaS / Web & Mobile Application.
**Business model:** Freemium (Free basic charting; Pro plans for advanced data/tools).

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Individual retail traders, prop firms, and professional analysis desks.
**Decision-makers:** Active Day Traders, Scalpers, and Technical Analysts.
**Primary use case:** Decoding market pressure (buying/selling) that standard candles hide.
**Jobs to be done:**
- "Hire" GoCharting to see where large institutional orders are being placed.
- "Hire" GoCharting to execute trades directly from charts across multiple brokers.
- "Hire" GoCharting to automate custom indicators using Lipi.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Standard charts only show price/time; they don't show the volume or intent (the "why") behind price moves.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Many platforms require expensive data plugins for Orderflow.
- Fragmented workflows (charting in one app, trading in another).
- High learning curve for scripting languages like PineScript or C++.
**Emotional tension:** Fear of "missing out" on big moves or getting trapped in a "fake out" because the volume data was hidden.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** TradingView (lacks deep Orderflow), QuantTower, Sierra Chart (complex UI), Bookmap.
**Secondary:** Broker-native platforms (e.g., Zerodha Kite, Fyers).
**How they fall short:** Most are either too simple (missing Delta/CVD) or too complex/dated in UI.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- **Lipi Scripting:** Easier, next-gen scripting for non-coders.
- **Unified Ecosystem:** Charting + Analysis + Execution in one browser tab.
- **Orderflow Specialist:** Native support for TFR (Trap Fade Reversal) and Absorption ratios.

## Customer Language
**Words to use:** Delta, CVD, Footprint, Absorption, Liquidity Grabs, Market Profile, Orderflow.
**Glossary:**
- **Lipi:** GoCharting’s proprietary scripting language.
- **CVD:** Cumulative Volume Delta (net buying vs. selling over time).

## Brand Voice
**Tone:** Technical, Authoritative, Empowering.
**Style:** Direct and performance-oriented.
**Personality:** The "Pro" tool that's actually accessible.

## Proof Points
**Metrics:** 2+ Million traders worldwide; 1 Million+ app downloads.
**Customers:** Integrations with Zerodha, Fyers, ByBit, Interactive Brokers, etc.

## Goals
**Business goal:** Growth in Pro-tier subscriptions and community engagement.
**Conversion action:** Download App / Join Discord / Pro Plan Signup.

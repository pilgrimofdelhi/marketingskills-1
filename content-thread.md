**Source:** Inspired by GoCharting's core YouTube themes.

Standard candles show you where price went. Orderflow shows you WHY it went there.

**Tweet 2 (The Problem):**
Candlesticks are lagging indicators. They are the result of a battle that has already finished.

To win, you need to see the battle *in real-time*. ⚔️

That’s where Cumulative Volume Delta (CVD) comes in.

**Tweet 3 (CVD Insight):**
CVD measures the net difference between aggressive buyers and aggressive sellers.

Price going up but CVD going down? That's **Bearish Divergence**. 🐻

Passive sellers are absorbing the aggressive buyers. A reversal is likely brewing.

**Tweet 4 (The Footprint):**
Footprint charts (Bid/Ask clusters) allow you to peek inside the candle.

Look for "Imbalances." When buying volume is 3x higher than selling volume at a specific price, you’ve found institutional interest. 🏦
